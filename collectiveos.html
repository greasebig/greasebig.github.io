Frequently Asked Questions

How does greasebig match researchers?+

Our AI engine analyzes over 40 data points including research areas, methodology, team size, geographic coverage, and past performance to recommend the highest-compatibility collaborators.

How long does it take to find a collaborator?+

Most researchers receive their first high-quality match within 48 hours of completing their profile. The average time to first published paper is 14 weeks.

What types of researchers use greasebig?+

We serve researchers of all sizes — from solo investigators to 200+ person labs — across computer vision, NLP, systems, and AI.

Is there a free plan available?+

Yes! Our Starter plan is free forever and includes up to 5 active collaborations, basic analytics, and full access to the researcher directory.

Can I manage projects directly on the platform?+

Absolutely. Our built-in project tracker lets you track research from idea to publication, assign team members, and calculate citation share — all in one place.
